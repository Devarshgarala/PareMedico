Our Manufacturing

We manufacture high-quality hospital linen at our family-owned facility in Gujarat. Every product goes through direct quality control from raw fabric selection to finished goods because accountability matters when it comes to patient care.

All products can be customized with your hospital logo, brand colors, and preferred dimensions. We work with hospitals, nursing homes, and healthcare chains across India and supply in bulk from a minimum order of 10000 pieces.

What We Make

Our Product Range

Six core product lines all manufactured in-house, all available for customization and bulk order.

Hospital Bedsheets

120–150 Thread Count · Cotton

Shrink-controlled weave available in multiple colors. Built for repeated industrial washing without losing shape or softness.

Patient Gowns

100% Cotton · Easy-Open

Designed for patient dignity and clinical ease. Available with front or back closure in standard and custom sizing.

Towels

400–500 GSM · High Absorbency

Premium GSM terry fabric for maximum absorbency. Maintains loft and absorbency even after heavy institutional washing cycles.

Industry Uniforms

Heavy-Duty Cotton Blend

Professional uniforms for hospital staff and healthcare workers. Durable, comfortable, and available with custom hospital branding.

Pillow Covers

Matching Sets · Envelope Closure

Coordinated sets with bedsheets available. Envelope closure design prevents pillow displacement during patient care.

Blankets

Lightweight · Washable

Thermal-balanced for patient comfort in air-conditioned environments. Machine washable and resistant to shrinkage.

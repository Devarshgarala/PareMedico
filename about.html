Our Story

Gujarat Based.
Globally Focused.

Paré Medico is a Gujarat-based healthcare solutions company, MSME registered and fully licensed for domestic supply and international export. We specialize in two high-demand verticals: hospital textiles and pharmaceutical & nutraceutical products.

Our hospital textiles division is backed by a family-owned manufacturing unit, giving us direct quality control at every stage. Our pharma & nutra division sources exclusively from GMP-certified manufacturers, ensuring every export meets the compliance standards of our destination markets.

We are not just a supplier we are a compliance-first, quality-driven partner for hospitals, distributors, and international buyers.
